Bish Bosh 2.0

Bish Bosh is a way to change some numbers to string in
a loop, in that case the user decides the loop limit and then the
dividend and the divisor in the equation and when the loop runs all the
numbers that fulfills those conditions turns to "Bish" or "Bosh" or "Bish-Bosh"
